Loop 3: Dream (the AI Steve Jobs you're asking for)
This is different from QA. This isn't "what's broken" — it's "what's missing that would make this transformative." This is the product vision loop.
This one is best run as a periodic conversation right here in Claude. Not a script — a dialogue. Once a week or whenever you have energy for it, come here with:

Here's what I built this week: [paste the Completed section of BACKLOG.md]
Here's what the app does now: [paste Current State from CLAUDE.md]
Here's what the product review found: [paste the latest review report]
Put on your Steve Jobs hat. What's the one thing this app should do next that would make everything else irrelevant? What am I not seeing? What would make someone use this every single day and never go back?

I'll give you one opinionated answer, not a list of 20 features. One bet. You evaluate it, and if it resonates, you add it to the backlog with a sprint scope.
